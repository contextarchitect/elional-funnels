3. Drinking More Water Makes It Worse

This is the clearest sign you're dealing with cortisol, not water retention. When you increase your water intake and the puffiness gets worse , or when cutting sodium makes no difference , that's your HPA axis dysfunction showing itself.

Elevated cortisol triggers aldosterone release, which makes your kidneys hold onto sodium and water. More water just gives your dysregulated system more fluid to retain.
